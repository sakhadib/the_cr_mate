is a revolutionary platform developed by dedicated to facilitating seamless communication between Class Representatives (CRs) and their fellow classmates. Our mission is to empower CRs in universities and colleges with a user-friendly interface that allows them to share academic notices, club announcements, classroom codes, and study materials effortlessly.

Key Features:
1. Exclusive CR Authority: CRs have the sole authority to publish and manage notices, ensuring accurate information reaches their classmates.
2. Unique University Code (UUC): Each CR receives a UUC to share only with fellow classmates, ensuring privacy and restricting access to university members only.
3. Secure Registration: CRs set a password during registration to protect their account and maintain information integrity.
